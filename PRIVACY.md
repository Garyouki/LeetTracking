# LeetTracking Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2, 2026**

LeetTracking is a Chrome extension that helps you with spaced repetition for
LeetCode problems. Your privacy is important. This policy explains what
information the extension handles and where it is stored.

## Data We Handle

LeetTracking **does not** collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on
our servers. By default, all data generated by the extension is stored in one of two
places:

1.  **On Your Local Machine (`chrome.storage.local`):**
    - Your saved LeetCode problems (cards), including review history and notes.
    - Your daily review statistics.
    - Your Notion integration tokens (if enabled).

2.  **In Your Personal Cloud Storage (`chrome.storage.sync`):**
    - Your extension settings, such as theme and daily limits.
    - Your GitHub Personal Access Token (if you enable GitHub Gist sync).

This data is managed by your browser and is not accessible to us.

### Personal Information We Collect

**Google Account Information:**
- We collect your Google email address through OAuth 2.0 authentication
- This is used solely as a unique user identifier for data synchronization
- Your email is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to our servers

**Third-Party Service Tokens (Optional):**
- GitHub Personal Access Token (for GitHub Gist sync)
- Notion API Token (for Notion database sync)
- These tokens are stored locally and only sent to their respective services

### Learning Data We Process

**LeetCode Problem Information:**
- Problem titles, difficulty levels, tags, and IDs
- Your review ratings and progress (Again, Hard, Good, Easy)
- Review scheduling data using FSRS algorithm
- Personal notes for each problem

**Usage Analytics:**
- Daily learning statistics and review history
- Card state information (new, learning, review, relearning)

### Optional GitHub Gist Sync

LeetTracking offers an optional feature to sync your data across browsers using
GitHub Gists. This feature is **disabled by default** and requires you to:

1. Create a GitHub Personal Access Token (PAT) with the `gist` scope.
2. Explicitly enable sync in the extension settings.

When enabled:

- Your extension data (cards, stats, notes, and settings) is uploaded to a
  **private** GitHub Gist that you own.
- Your PAT is stored locally in your browser and is sent only to GitHub's API
  (`api.github.com`) to authenticate sync requests.
- **We never receive or have access to your PAT or synced data.**

You can disable this feature at any time, and your Gist will remain under your
control on GitHub.

### Optional Notion Integration

LeetTracking offers optional integration with Notion databases. This feature is
**disabled by default** and requires you to:

1. Create a Notion Integration token with appropriate permissions.
2. Provide your Notion database ID.
3. Explicitly enable sync in the extension settings.

When enabled:

- Your learning data (problem information, ratings, notes) is sent to your
  personal Notion database.
- Your Notion token is stored locally and only sent to Notion's API
  (`api.notion.com`) for authentication.
- **We never receive or have access to your Notion token or database.**

You can disable this feature at any time, and your Notion database remains
under your control.

## Data Usage and Sharing

If you use GitHub Gist sync, your data is shared only with GitHub
under their privacy policy. If you use Notion integration, your data is shared
only with Notion under their privacy policy. For Google authentication,
your email is shared only with Google's OAuth 2.0 service.

Aside from these optional integrations, your data is not shared with
any other party.

## Data Security

- All sensitive tokens are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage
- Tokens are only transmitted to their respective service providers over HTTPS
- We do not store or have access to any third-party service credentials
- You can revoke all third-party permissions at any time

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all data we store about you (through the extension's export feature)
- Delete all your data (through the extension's reset feature)
- Revoke third-party service permissions at any time
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this
page with an updated date.
